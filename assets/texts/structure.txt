    First screen of app is Login page. To use this app you need to register new account or log in into existing one. The main screen of our app is the screen with groups. To see the author of group and its description tap on the card and hold til the dialog appear.  In groups you can find shared folders and files. In the bottom of each page you can see button Add to add group/folder/file. On settings page you can find and theme mode and your login. Also there you can log out from account.
